FAQ

Common Questions from Pet Parents

Everything you need to know before visiting Noah Animal Clinic in Hyderabad.

Do I need a prior appointment? +
While walk-ins are welcome, booking an appointment helps us ensure Dr. Ranjith is ready for your pet without any wait. You can book via phone, WhatsApp, or the form on this page.
Do you provide emergency care? +
Yes. Emergency support is available at the clinic during working hours (9 AM – 9 PM). For guidance outside these hours, please call 76720 55007 directly.
Do you treat birds and exotic pets? +
Absolutely. Dr. Ranjith Kumar has significant experience with parrots, birds, and exotic small animals. We provide specialist consultations for non-traditional pets.
Do you provide vaccination services? +
Yes. We offer complete vaccination and immunisation programmes for dogs, cats, birds, and puppies/kittens following evidence-based schedules.
Can I consult Dr. Ranjith online? +
Yes. Tele-consultation is available. Contact us on WhatsApp at 76720 55007 to arrange a virtual consultation with Dr. Ranjith Kumar.
What should I bring for my pet's first visit? +
Please bring any previous vaccination records or medical history, your pet in a carrier or on a leash, and a list of any symptoms or concerns you've noticed. Our team will guide you through the rest.
Do you handle surgical cases? +
Yes. We handle soft tissue surgeries, spay/neuter procedures, gynaecological surgeries, and other interventional procedures at our facility. Please call to discuss your pet's specific case.
Do you have an in-house lab? +
Yes. Noah Animal Clinic has an in-house laboratory for blood panels, urinalysis, cytology, and other rapid diagnostics so you get results quickly without external delays.
What are your clinic timings? +
The clinic is open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. For urgent queries outside these hours, please reach out via the emergency number.
How can I contact the clinic quickly? +
The fastest ways to reach us are: Call 📞 76720 55007, WhatsApp 💬 76720 55007, or visit us at D.No. 12/19/33/1, Green Hills Road, Moosapet, Hyderabad 500018.
